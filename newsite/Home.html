Could you be a volunteer?

We are looking for volunteers to collect none perishable food and living essentials from their local community who we ask to leave out on the first Saturday of the month.  

These donations are given to charities and community groups who play an integral role in providing food aid to the most vulnerable in the community. 

Volunteers commit to a single month at a time.  Leaflets are provided to which can be collected and posted during the five days prior to let your neighbours know you will be collecting on event day.

Please select an area:

Gosforth & Wideopen north Tyneside Walker

Please note, registration forms open for the next date a few days after each collection and close 10 days before to allow for planning.  If registration is currently closed you can REGISTER YOUR INTEREST today and we will email you when it reopens.
