where do donations go?

We can only collect in the streets with a registered volunteer which can vary greatly month on month. Some volunteers collect every month without fail, some every other month, some once a quarter and others, just the once.  Therefore the number of charities and communitiy groups we support can vary month on month.  A full list of all the organisations we have supported since July 2020 can be found HERE and testimonials HERE

Saturday 4th November

All donations collected went to: ​Building Futures East, Cedarwood Trust, Hazlerigg Community Centre,  Our Community Spirit, Walking With in North Tyneside and Wallsend Memorial  Hall.  

Saturday 7th October

All donations collected went to: ​Building Futures East, Cedarwood Trust, Hazlerigg Community Centre,  Our Community Spirit, Walking With in North Tyneside and Wallsend Memorial  Hall.  

Saturday 2nd September

A special thank you to the Operations Team; Catherine Scorer, Rozy Rozza, Michelle Dixon, Dawn Ancrum, Lisa Williams and Jill Politch who consistently support me making this possible each Saturday, ensuring food gets safely from our volunteers to the charities and community groups on the very same day!! I appreciate you all so much.

Saturday 5th August

96 volunteers covering 193 streets!  All donations collected went to: ​Building Futures East, Cedarwood Trust, Hazlerigg Community Centre,  Our Community Spirit, Walking With in North Tyneside and Wallsend Memorial  Hall.  

Saturday 1st July

We celebrate our 3rd Year Anniversary!  All donations collected went to: ​Building Futures East, Cedarwood Trust, Hazlerigg Community Centre,  Our Community Spirit, Walking With in North Tyneside and Wallsend Memorial  Hall.
