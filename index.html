Tin On A Wall
North Tyneside CIC
Company Number: 14799578

We are a volunteer lead Community Interest Company who's volunteers collect non-perishable food and household essentials every first Saturday of the month (except January) for a number of charities, food banks and community groups in North Tyneside and Newcastle upon Tyne.  We invite residents to register each month to leaflet and collect in their own street/streets, so the volume of donations collected will vary month on month.

Campaign Recognition

Making a Difference

Jamie - Whitley Bay

Thank you Jamie from Whitley Bay for his lovely drawing attached to his food donation on Saturday 2nd February.  A few volunteers gathered together in intrigue, opening the little picture window and the envelope on the back.  We loved solving your puzzle using your carefully thought out colour coding.

Spirit of North Tyneside Award for "Caring for People in your Community."

I'm proud to announce we have won the Spirit of North Tyneside Award for "Caring for People in your community." Thank you to the 24 people who voted, including our Tin On A Wall volunteer Nicki Willis from Preston Grange, who joined me, Jo Scorer in collecting the award on Friday 15th December.

It was wonderful that many of the other winners there say they'd recently donated or volunteered....the Young Major presenting was actually the Police Cadet dressed as Santa just a few weeks ago!!!  Our campaign is certainly making waves in the community, and word is still spreading over 3.5 years on.
