Supporting the Homeless at Christmas

Living on the streets can be a lonely time for someone who is homeless, often having no contact with friends or family. During the festive season when 'we' are surrounded by our loved ones in our warm and safe place, the homeless are not only battling loneliness but the weather, illness, starvation and many associated dangers when vulnerable - can you imagine, I can't!

"Our army of Tin On A Wall volunteers,collect food donations every month for a number of charities and community groups, BUT in December, our volunteers went even further!!!  They made up gifts for the Destiny StreetWorx Christmas Shoe Box/Gift Bag Appeal, for men, women and dogs. Giving the homeless a small gift at Christmas it shows that someone cares.  Our volunteers gifted over 60 BOXES / BAGS! I was lucky enough to go out with the charity on 23rd December to give these out to the homeless.

Thank you all so so much, you are all such lovely people. We also gave them almost 100 selection boxes too! " 

Jo Scorer - Dec 2022
